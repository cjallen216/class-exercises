Did You Know?

After the show was canceled, the chopper was sold to a German firm and became an air ambulance.

The Airwolf helicopter is based on the Sikorsky X2 the world's fastest helicopter.

The production needed special permission from the FAA for Airwolf to fly with its weapons deployed, even though they were props.
